# Business units module

This module owns shops, branches, warehouses, sales desks, and other business units.

Business units are the lowest operational scope for workers and sales activity.

## Files

- `mod.rs` exports the business unit module files.
- `model.rs` defines the business unit model.
- `repository.rs` will contain SQLx queries for business units.
- `service.rs` will contain unit creation and access rules.
